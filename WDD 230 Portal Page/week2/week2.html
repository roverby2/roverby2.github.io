Design Principles

Repetition

Udemy

This website shows the principle of repetition with the courses that students have most viewed within a certain amount of time. The layout is the same for all of them that way they can group up and catch the viewers attention.

Go to Udemy

Contrast

Youtube Side Menu

This website shows the principle of contrast on the white title and with black background and in turn, makes it extremely easy to notice and read for the average viewer or visiter.

Go to Youtube

Hick's Law

Netflix New User Page

This website limits the users choices so they are directed to sign up and subscribe to their service. In this case, it catches the viewer with the big bold text advertising what they have and right below it is a box to enter your email and get started with their service.

Go to Netflix
